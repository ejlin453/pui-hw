Cynthia Visits California
5/18/2021 - 5/22/2021



Memory:
In the beginning of my college freshman year summer, my close college friend Cynthia visited California. During her time here, me and some CMU friends brought her to many different places to experience. We brought her to a shopping mall we frequent, where we tried on clothes and bought Legos together. She also tried In-n-Out burgers for the first time and we visited a viewpoint, where we gazed at the stars and the city lights of the valley. Two notable days were when we went to the Academy of Sciences and the Boardwalk, where we visited an aquarium and played in the cool, Californian beach. This multi-day visit gave me many memories and strengthened bonds with my friends.
