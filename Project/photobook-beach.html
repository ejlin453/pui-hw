Winter Sunset Beach
12/20/2022



Memory:
On this day, I visited a local beach with some of my high school friends in California. We went to watch the sunset and take some pictures, although we spent half the time messing around and not taking each other seriously. That day, the beach was quite empty, which gave us an environment of peace and serenity. We spent a lot of time running around, throwing sand at each other, taking photos of each other, taking selfies, and catching up. The most notable sight this day was the reflection of the sky on the seawater-covered sand, providing us some unique shots. It is safe to say that on this day, I witnessed one of the most beautiful sunsets I've ever seen.
