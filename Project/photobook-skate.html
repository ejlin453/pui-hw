Skating



Memory:
One day in the first semester of my freshman year of college, I borrowed my friend's cruiser board for a few days and found the experience thrilling. Once I got the hang of the basics, I bought my own board began skating on campus to classes. After two months, I found myself skating every day. I realized I found a new hobby I was genuinely passionate about.

Over the next few years, I would purchase a variety of higher quality boards and learn new skills. I would relax and cruise comfortably around new flat neighborhoods or be pumped with adrenaline skating down hills. Because of skating, I found an unmatched sense of passion and freedom.
