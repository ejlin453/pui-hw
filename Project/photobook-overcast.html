Overcast Beach
5/13/2023



Memory:
On this day during the summer, I visited a local beach in California with my childhood friends to take pictures. This was the first day of exploring my new hobby of photography. We spent most of the day walk around the beach and hiking the nearby trail, snapping pictures with our digital scenery and each other. Although there was a lot of overcast and we spent half the time messing around with each other, some of the photos turned out really good. This day gave me fond memories of catching up with my California friends and starting my journey in photography.
