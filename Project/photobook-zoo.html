During the summer of my sophomore year of college, I became interested in my friend, Celine, and asked her out on a few dates. On this day 8/23/2023, we visited the Pittsburgh Zoo and Aquarium together and I decided this was the day I officially ask her to be my girlfriend. We had a lot of fun together and got closer, and at the end of the day we started dating. As such, I believe this day was a big turning point in my college life.
