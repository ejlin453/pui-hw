Last Night in E-Tower
5/8/2022



Memory:
In my freshman year at Carnegie Mellon University, I lived with my childhood friend, Alex, in the campus dorm Morewood E-Tower. Throughout the year, I made countless core memories in this room 311. Here we had small get togethers, heart-to-heart talks, dinners, gaming sessions, and study sessions. Because I study better in my own room, I would often stay up studying on my own in this room, racking up the hours I spend in E-Tower. On this day 5/8/2022, Alex and I began packing up to move out of E-Tower as the semester came to an end. Some close floormates of ours came in to hang out a bit and we decided to take some fun pictures together to commemorate the time we had. These photos embody the fun and friendships we made in E-Tower.
