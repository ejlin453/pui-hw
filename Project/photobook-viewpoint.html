Viewpoint
12/28/2022



Memory:
On this day, my high school friends Ashley and Ryan decided to hold a photoshoot at a local viewpoint. At this viewpoint, we took many photos using a professional camera. We got beautiful shots of the landscape, which was covered in peaceful fields of yellow grass. I distinctly remember the brisk air of the Californian winter as the sun set. This particular memory fulfilled a unique photo concept that I would not have experienced otherwise.
