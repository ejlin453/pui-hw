Lambda Banquet
12/3/2023



Memory:
On this day, my fraternity Lambda Phi Epsilon held an end-of-semester banquet at Grand Concourse to celebrate another semester of brotherhood. The venue was stunning, which gave us many beautiful photos. At my table, I sat with my girlfriend, my childhood friend, and other close friends and brothers. A notable memory from this banquet was my table's energy and our mission to drink as many bottles of premium water, which resulted in many chaotic and hilarious moments. I received a paddle from my little, which was designed to reflect my passion for skateboarding. Overall, this experience was a lot more fun than I anticipated and I left with many moments to cherish.
