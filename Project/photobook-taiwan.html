Taiwan National Park
7/17/2022 - 7/18/2022



Memory:
During my visit in Taiwan during my college freshman year summer, my entire family and I visited a national park for a few days. Here I was able to experience the deep green nature of the island, which was a stark contrast to the bustling city life of Taipei, Taiwan where stayed. During this trip, I got to spend a lot more time with my relatives, who I rarely see since they live in Taiwan. I distinctly remember the humidity, the chitter of cicadas, and the laughter of my family. This trip allowed me to catch up with my family and connect with Taiwan's natural environment.
