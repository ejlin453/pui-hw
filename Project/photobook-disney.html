Disney with Cousins
1/16/2023 - 1/17/2023



Memory:
During the winter break of my college sophomore year, I went to Disneyland with two of my cousins from Taiwan. On this trip, I was able to catch up with my cousins who I rarely get to see. I also got to revisit many childhood memories, since the last time I went to Disneyland was in elementary school. I got to see how much the amusement park changed and relive fond memories by going on rides I frequented as a child such as Star Tours. Going during the wintertime gave me a unique experience since their winter features and Christmas lights were still up. This trip allowed me to reconnect with my cousins and fully relive old memories while making new ones.
