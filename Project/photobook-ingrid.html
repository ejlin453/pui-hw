Photoshoot with Ingrid
8/19/2022



Memory:
This was the first time I saw Ingrid in over a year. Ingrid is a childhood friend of mine I was really close to through the end of high school. After we both went off to college, we unfortunately drifted a part a bit. On this day, we met up at a local mall in California where we had a photoshoot for fun. We matched outfit concepts and tried going for a certain aesthetic. However, we spent a lot of time messing around with each other and catching up (as evident in the photos). I am thankful I had this day to reconnect with my good friend and take noteworthy photos to commemorate it.
