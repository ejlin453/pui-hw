Chicago



Memory:
After my brother graduated college and moved to Chicago for his full-time job, I got to visit and explore Chicago pretty often. The lively city life of the Loop became a familiar environment and each visit made new memories. I was there to help my brother first move into his apartment, where I met and bonded with his suitemate's cat (which I evidently spent a lot of time with). Whenever I had a break in college, I would visit for a few days and explore new restaurants, meet new people, and see new places.
