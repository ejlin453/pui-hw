Puerto Rico
10/13/2023 - 10/18/2023



Memory:
For my Fall Break of my college junior year, I went on a trip to Puerto Rico with my friends. Initially, I was skeptical about going, but this 6-day trip ended up being one of my most fond college memories. The trip comprised of eight of the closest friends I made at Carnegie Mellon University since freshman year. Because of them, this trip was filled with fun moments filled with laughter, growth, and bonding.


There are numerous notable moments that made this trip especially memorable. We visited many notable landmarks, restaurants, beaches, and more. One location we visited was Old San Juan, where we enjoyed the town’s landscape, buildings, and food. The beautiful beaches left us unwilling to leave the relaxation of the ,mild waves and warm seawater. This trip allowed me to experience and connect with Puerto Rico’s culture and natural environment.
